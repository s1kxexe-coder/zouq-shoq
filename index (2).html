Our Story

A Quaidabad staple, one daig at a time.

Zouq Shoq began as a small pakwaan counter on the roadside, run on one belief — real biryani takes time. Rice soaked properly, meat marinated overnight, and every daig sealed with dough and left on dum until the aroma tells you it's ready.

Today the counter has grown, but the method hasn't changed. Families in Quaidabad still stop by for the same beef and chicken biryani, karahi, and BBQ that built this shop's name — cooked fresh, served hot, no compromises.
